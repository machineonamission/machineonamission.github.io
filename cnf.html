What is Rēticīvis?

Rēticīvis is a Latin neologism coined by O, Eheu that means "citizen of the internet" or "netizen". It is a direct combination of "rēte" (net) and "cīvis" (citizen).

What is the Rēticīvis logo?

Designed by a friend of mine in my college's graphic design class, the logo combines 3 elements: the <tagname> style tags used in HTML, the letter 𝑥 as used in algebra, and an infinity symbol.
